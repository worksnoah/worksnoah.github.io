Component 3: Solution Design

Solution Overview

In this phase of the project, the objective is to design a solution that addresses the critical issues faced by vessels operating in remote areas without internet connectivity. The proposed solution is a ruggedized, portable device that provides GPS and essential offline resources to aid in navigation and emergency situations.

Project Requirements

  • Durability: The device must withstand rough handling and harsh conditions.
  • Offline Capability: The device should operate independently of internet access.
  • Portability: Compact and easy to transport.
  • Battery Life: Long-lasting battery to support extended use without charging.
  • Functionality: Includes essential features like GPS, a library of maps, and offline Wikipedia access.
