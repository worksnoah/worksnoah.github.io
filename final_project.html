Component 2: Problem Validation

In particular, it identifies North America as a major growth region because of the various technological advancements and computing manufacturing giants located here. Panasonic, Dell, Getac, and Durabook are also recognized as the industry leaders. This article validates the demand for a rugged portable computer by showing that a market already exists and that it will continue to grow in the coming years. Finally, the article doesn’t provide any specifics as to what the most popular kinds of rugged portable computers are, like handhelds or laptops.

Patent Reviews

Patent Review: US9010623B2

Source: Arnouse, M. (2015). Portable computing system and portable computer for use with same (U.S. Patent No. US9010623B2). Washington, DC: U.S. Patent and Trademark Office.

Summary

The patent describes a small, portable computing system comprising a pocket-sized personal computer and a companion reader device. The pocket PC includes flash memory, optional processors, and GPS capabilities, fitting into a compact form similar to a credit card for easy portability. An important feature is its integration with the reader device, which provides essential components like a monitor, keyboard, and USB connectors necessary for user interaction. Security is enhanced with features such as password protection, fingerprint authentication, and RFID technology, ensuring data security even in public settings. Overall, it highlights the system's innovation in compact computing with robust security, suitable for various personal and professional applications.

Critique

This article highlights the use of a miniature monitor and keyboard, and demonstrates several features that could be used in our specific use case. However, it doesn’t mention anything about the battery life. Additionally, it doesn’t mention resistance against moisture and saltwater, which could be a problem on boats. It also fails to mention how they will fit a whole keyboard, monitor, and computer into a pocket-sized device, leading this patent to sound like speculation.

Patent Review: US10660232B1

Source: Arnouse, M. (Inventor). (2020). Portable computing system with advanced security features (U.S. Patent No. US10660232B1). Washington, DC: U.S. Patent and Trademark Office.

Summary

This patent is about a portable and mobile data center, which in the images is built into a case like the one we’d like to use. It has functions like computing, data storage, communications, and routing. The specific rugged case is suitable for harsh environments, and it has hot swappable power supplies to quickly replace the battery. It also offers a coupling design for the power supplies to daisy chain them. Additionally, the case supposedly protects against electromagnetic interference.

Critique

This patent uses easy removal and deployment of both computing and power systems, allowing it to be dynamic and flexible. Additionally, the idea of an integrated power supply allows for a streamlined design. However, the design is complex with multiple racks and mechanisms. Additionally, it is specific to mobile data centers, which doesn’t fit our use case.

Patent Review: EP2630552B1

Source: Reber, D., & Garel, M. (Inventors). (2018). Portable computing device and method with modular expansion (European Patent No. EP2630552B1). Munich, Germany: European Patent Office.

Summary

This patent is for a tablet computer with a sealed waterproof compartment for the electronics. It also includes an outward facing touch screen display with the electronics of the display inside the sealed compartment. Additionally, the battery is integrated to the case, and there is an active cooling system that transfers heat to the ambient air. Finally, there is a heatsink and fan to facilitate heat dissipation.

Critique

The active cooling system is similar to the idea we have planned, as it uses a fan and heatsink to cool the components. Additionally, it uses a sealed waterproof compartment for the electronics, just like ours. However, the patent still has the screen exposed, which could lead it to crack or get wet. Also, it might not be able to dissipate the heat effectively, because of the small surface area. Finally, the patent uses metal to connect components, which is more susceptible to vibration issues than plastic, as well as heat penetration.

Component 3: Solution Design

Project Requirements

  • The device will feature magnetic USB-C ports to make connections more secure and durable, reducing wear and tear.
  • A built-in tool drawer will be included for easy access to essential tools, making repairs or adjustments more convenient.
  • A rugged button will be implemented to switch between screens, allowing for a simple and reliable user interface in harsh environments.
  • The battery pack will be swappable, with one magnetic plug, to enable quick and easy battery replacement while maintaining a secure connection.
  • The interior will provide dedicated storage for small tools, ensuring that they remain accessible and organized during transport.
  • An exhaust fan will be installed to prevent overheating and ensure stable performance during extended use.
  • A physical library of SD card operating systems will be integrated to facilitate switching between different system environments offline.
  • The battery life will support 24 hours of continuous use, ensuring the device remains operational for long periods without recharging.
  • The device will be waterproof when the container is closed, and the internal components will be water-resistant to handle exposure to moisture.
  • The case will float on water, preventing the device from sinking if dropped overboard or in water environments.
  • A secondary cursor input method will be included besides the touchscreen, allowing for more flexibility in input options.
  • A dedicated drawer or container will be built for SD card storage, providing easy access and organization.
  • A fast-charge USB-C cable with a retractable mechanism will be used for input/output, allowing for quick and efficient charging.
  • The device will feature a full-size mechanical keyboard for enhanced typing comfort and durability, even in harsh environments.
  • Built-in speakers will provide audio output for media playback or system notifications, ensuring full functionality without external devices.
