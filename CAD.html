Final Project CAD Description

For my final project, I designed a rugged cyberdeck using Onshape CAD software. The purpose is to build a portable, waterproof PC inside a Pelican case, optimized for use on a boat with offline access to GPS, maps, and Wikipedia. I used materials like ASA carbon fiber for the outer frame and TPU for internal mounts, focusing on durability and functionality.
