Computer Controlled Cutting

Exploring designs created using CNC and laser cutting techniques.

Design 1: Cactus Coaster Laser Cut

The first two images showcase my cactus coaster design, which is a set of laser-cut coasters that can be assembled to form a complete cactus shape. This design emphasizes modularity and creativity, allowing each coaster to serve as both a functional piece and part of a larger artistic project. I chose this design to demonstrate the precision of laser cutting and the potential for custom, interactive products that combine utility with visual appeal. The potted plant base can be 3d-printed to complete the effect.

Design 2: Noah Works Vinyl Cutter Logo

I chose this logo to represent the combination of mechanical design and innovation. The gear symbolizes engineering and precision, while the broken gear represents problem-solving and creative solutions. The name 'Noah Works' is included to emphasize the hands-on nature of my work, highlighting that I bring ideas to life through practical, detailed engineering.
