Molding and Casting

Molding

This section showcases my work in creating silicone molds for casting resin and other materials. I designed and 3D printed the mold negatives, ensuring proper venting and alignment. The silicone was carefully mixed and poured to capture fine details, demonstrating the precision and repeatability molding allows in manufacturing.

Casting

In this section, I explore different casting techniques, including resin casting. Using the silicone molds I created, I cast high-detail resin parts, such as a Benchy and another small object. The resin maintains fine details from the mold, proving the effectiveness of the molding process.
